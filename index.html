Steven Eiselen presents: Conway's Game Of Life!

Instructions:

  • Enter the 'p' key to pause/unpause the simulation
  • Enter the 'r' key to reset the world with a new set of automatons
  • Enter the 'c' key to remove (clear) all of the world's automatons
  • Enter the 'm' key to change modes between wrap-around and solid room
  • Click on a cell with the left mouse button to toggle its on/off state
    • Note: this works better when the simulation is paused!
